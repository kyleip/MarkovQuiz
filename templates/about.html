How are the made up sentences generated?

They are made with a process called Markov chains. The machine will make a sentence based on the probability of when words occur in relation to each other. This is the same technology used in predictive texts for mobile phones.

Why Ulysses ?

I thought it was quite difficult when I read it, so I thought it would be interesting to see a machine try to mimic such a distinct writing style. I think because the novel uses interesting sentence structures, the quiz can be quite difficult!
